Namaste

Namaste from Sagar. I have developed this App which can encode any password, message or secret chat and can be decoded only by you. With just a key (your favourite number) you can covert normal message to encrypted script (not readable) and bring it back with the same key.
The main motive behind creating this app is to solve problem of remembering all username and passwords which get mixed up in our minds. It is too risky to use storage apps as Notes and Locker apps are getting hacked any time these days I needed to do this.

How to ENCRYPT:-

From menu select app. You need to give 2 inputs. 1'st is key and 2'nd is msg you want to encrypt. Press ENCRYPT to encrpt msg. In ouput you have encrypted form of your message.

How to DECRYPT:-

Copy encypted msg to MSG. Give key in KEY section. Now press Decrypt. You got your msg back!!
